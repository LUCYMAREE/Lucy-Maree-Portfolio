Welcome

Hello! I'm Lucy McNay, for web design we had to make a website of something we enjoy. The thing that I enjoy is Design. This website is a place where you can find all of my Graphic design work.

About Me

On this website you will find my personal portfolio for graphic design, over the period of the last 5 years. I first started taking design as a course in high school in year 10 when I was 15 years old and have done it ever since. Over the years I have taken different photography, graphic design and general art.

I have learnt many things over the years and have improved in my design work in many different ways. By visiting this page, you will be able to see the improvements in my work and how much I have learned over the years.

This website is going to be updated with my latest work, and I will continue to update it in the future. By the end of my communication design course there will be all the work that I completed over the 3 years on this website as well as my high school work.
